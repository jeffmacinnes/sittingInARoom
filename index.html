I am sitting in a browser
an homage to Alvin Lucier in celebration of his 90th birthday
Performances of I am sitting in a room traditionally showcase the resonant frequencies of the space in which it's staged. This version substitutes physical space for the browser.

The relationship between the browser and the webpage defines the characteristics of the delay on each subsequent iteration.
Since this relationship is unique for each viewer, the resulting audio (and visualization) represents a unique performance reflecting the device on which it was performed.
